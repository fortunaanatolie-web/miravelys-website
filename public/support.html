Support

We’re here to help with Miravelys — calmly and clearly.

Email us

For account, subscription, privacy, or bug reports:

support@miravelys.com

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Do not send sensitive journal content unless you choose to.

Subscriptions & Restore

  • Buy Premium in the app via Apple. Prices come from the App Store for your region.
  • Use Restore Purchases on the Premium screen or in Settings if you reinstalled or switched devices (same Apple ID).
  • Cancel or change auto-renew in iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com.

Privacy & data

  • Your reflections stay on your device by default. See the Privacy Policy.
  • Export and delete controls live under Privacy in Miravelys Settings and are always free.
  • Local-only mode keeps assistance on-device when you prefer that.

If you need urgent help

Miravelys is not a crisis service. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services. In the app, crisis / safety resources remain reachable without Premium.
